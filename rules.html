Server Rules

Keep it simple: Don't be a jerk. But here are the specifics:

01

Respect Everyone

No harassment, bullying, or being toxic. We're here to have fun, not deal with drama. Treat people how you'd want to be treated.

02

No Spam

Don't flood channels with messages, emojis, or pings. Use channels for their intended purpose. Memes are cool, spam is not.

03

Keep It Appropriate

No NSFW content, excessive profanity, or anything that would get us in trouble. Keep it PG-13 at worst.

04

No Self-Promo

Don't advertise your stuff, other servers, or try to poach members. This server is for the squad, not for your side hustle.

05

Listen to Mods

If a mod tells you to chill, chill. They're here to keep things running smooth. Arguing with mods = bad idea.

06

Use Channels Correctly

Game talk in game channels, general stuff in general. It's organized for a reason - use it right.

07

Voice Channel Etiquette

Don't be loud/annoying, don't play music without asking, don't interrupt people constantly. Basic respect goes a long way.

08

No Drama

Keep personal beef out of the server. Handle it in DMs. We're not here to referee your arguments.

Breaking Rules = Consequences

First time: Warning. Second time: Timeout. Third time: Kick. Keep it up: Ban.

We're pretty chill, but don't test us. Just follow the rules and we're good.
